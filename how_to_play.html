How to Begin


if you just got to know AnimeForce this is where you should be!!


Building My Character


When you join the group, the first thing to do is to message an admin for a character card. You would be asked questions and given some options, such options can be found at other pages on the site, such as races & classes.


What is a Character Card?

A character card is your game identity. It shows your skills and details. You are adviced not to send your charcater card to any other players in the game.


What choices do i have to make for my Character Card?

you are required to choose the following


  • Character Name -- choose a character name for character (no already existing character name unless you use a different first or last name)
  • Age -- choose an age for your character (14 to 30 recommended)
  • Size -- your size will be determined by the race you chose. view all sizes here
  • gender -- you select your gender, male or female, no traps pls....
  • height/weight -- your height and weight is randomized by a calculator base on your race
  • Character description -- this basically means the color of your eyes, skin and hair, although all these will be available when the avatar maker is out
  • Ability Scores/stats -- your ability score is 5 on all sections, then you are then given 30 points for you to place the scores where they are wanted. see more here
  • Alignment -- your alignment describes your characters stand in society and how they view things, either good, bad or neutral but you basically get it from your background. read more about alignment here
  • languages -- your language is given to you based on your race, background or sometimes your class
  • country -- your country is your originated site, you select a country from the list of countries here
  • background -- you choose a particular background, meaning past life, you select one from there and you randomly get your personality traits, ideals, bond and flaws. it also determine part of your start equipment
  • Race -- you then select your race, you race determines your HP, CP and speed. it also gives you additional stats and special skills
  • class -- this is your characters method of fighting, class gives you features/perks as you level up
  • skills -- your basic skills apart from punch kick and kkg which are gotten from your race, are generated at random, you get to acquire new skills and also disband old ones.

you can see more about all these listed above at the Avatar Section


What are Skills?


These are what you use to battle. Skills can also be bought. There are special moves(KKG) which are given to you by your race. More about skills here.


Health & ChargePoints(Hp & Cp)


Your Health is the amount of damage your character can withstand before you are killed, while your Cp is the amount of energy you have. Cp is required to perform most skills and techniques.


Buying, Selling & Trading


There are several in-game currencies: XP, Prism, Gold, Silver, & Bronze. Xp is used to acquire skills and can be bought using real money(visit the shop for more info), while the others are used to buy or build/craft equipment or items. Items can be traded for other items. To do any of the above, message an admin.


Leveling up


You can increase your level by gaining Exp(Experience Points). Exp is gained when you win battles and tasks. Exp can also be lost through the same metric, but you cannot drop below your attained level when you lose Exp. Once you reach a new level your Exp counter begins from 0 again


Levels and Experience Points


  • Level 1 - 0Exp
  • Level 2 - 300Exp
  • Level 3 - 700Exp
  • Level 4 - 1500Exp
  • Level 5 - 2600Exp e.t.c.

Battle


In battle you make use of actions, bonus actions and reactions, normally skills use an action and an action is like a turn. some skills require bonus actions meaning they can be used even you have or not used your action. while reations are using bonus actions in opponents turn.
