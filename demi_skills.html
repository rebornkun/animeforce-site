Demi Humans Skills


Punch & Kick are the first skills owned by a demi humans, punch take 3hp and kicks take 2hp, They cannot be sold and they require no cp to use.



  1. claw strike(atk)- deal 3 damage to a target , this skill is regarded as a physical skill .1cp. 20xp

  2. beast frenzy(gain)- your speed increases by 30.2cp. 20x

  3. beast call(nom)- spawn a creature of your kkg type. 4cp

  4. poision fang(atk)- deal 2 damage to a target for 3 of their turns , at the begining of the turns, 4cp, 35xp

  5. secure aim(nom)- use a skill right now, this skill cant be neglected or destroyed no matter what. 2cp. 35xp
