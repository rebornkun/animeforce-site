Saiyan Race Modes/Transformations


Super Saiyan 1

Category: transformation
xp required: 100

Star:

Requirements: atleast lv 3, ?cp
Preforming time: 1 action
New Stats (height e.t.c):
Duration: until users cp is lesser than 5

while in this mode, all damage you take from skills are reduced by 1, all damage you deal with physical or your race skills are increased by 1 and your speed is increased by 20ft.

Super Saiyan 2

Category: transformation
xp required: 200

Star:

Requirements: atleast lv 5, ?cp
Preforming time: 1 action
New Stats (height e.t.c):
Duration: until users cp is lesser than 3

while in this mode, all damage you take from skills are reduced by 2, all damage you deal with physical or your race skills are increased by 3, you gain +1 mod each for Str, Con and Dex ability and your speed is increased by 40ft.
below is a table that shows the amount of cp spent for this transformation base on your lv.

Saiyan lv CP used
1-5 3cp every end turn
6-10 2cp every end turn
11-15 1cp every end turn
16-25 0.5cp every end turn
26>>> 5
