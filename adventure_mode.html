Details


Mining is the extraction of valuable minerals or other geological materials from the earth, usually through mines.
in Anime force before entering mines, you will see a sign board which indicates the rank, the entry fee and number of floors you can go in that particular mine.

The lower the floor you go, the more valuable the resources get, but be careful, it also means you see more dangerous monsters
In Mines things can happen like;

  • Monsters encounters - anytime you try to drill resources in a fall, sometimes you might encounter creatures. the creatures might have spotted you first or you spot the creature first, this will be noted in the roll. if you spot the creature first, you and all players can make a Dex(Stealth) Saving throw to escape the creature without being noticed. the Str check DC is either the creatures Passive perception or something else. if at least one person fails the check, the creature notices the players and a battle starts with the creature starting it. they might be more than one creature but the creature is not generated base on numbers of players or ranks but by mine rank and floor level. to escape after the battle starts, you can sacrifice you whole turn and roll another saving throw but + the floor number to the DC, you escape alone on a success with your already mined loot, on a fail, you lose that turn.
  • Gas leaks - a gas leak might also pop up while mining, this gas leak might affect the whole mine or only that floor, when it does, the amount of time players have to leave either the floor or mine is stated and also the damage the players which failed to escape before the explosion is occurs.

Also note that some mines in some places have special materials that can only be found there and like always special creatures to go with em... hehehe


Preparing to enter a mine

in preparation of entering a mine you need

  • Entry fee
  • Miner tools for each player which expires after every mine
  • Pick axe. note that some pick axe might not be able to mine some ores and minerals, so take the best one. also pickaxe and mostly all equipment have the durability which is How many times they can be used, meaning 1 durability = 1 mine time.
  • Weapons and items
